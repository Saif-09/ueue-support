What is ueue

ueue is the calm home for everything you save and never get back to. Tap Share in any app — a video, an article, a reel, a podcast, a post — and it lands in ueue. Sort it into a category, and ueue brings it back to you at the time you choose, in a focused session. No endless "watch later" pile.

Private by design

ueue keeps everything on your device. No account, no sign-up, no tracking — and nothing is ever sent to a server. It works fully offline.

FAQ

How do I save a link?

In any app, tap the Share button and choose ueue. Pick a category (or leave it in your Inbox), optionally set a reminder, and tap Save. You can also tap “Add a link” inside ueue and paste a URL.

Where do my saved links go?

Links you sort into a category appear in that category’s list and come back in its scheduled sessions. Anything you don’t categorise waits in your Inbox for triage.

How do reminders work?

Each category can have its own schedule (for example, Learning every weekday at 8 PM). You can also set a one-off reminder on any single link — as a normal notification or a time-sensitive alert.

Are my links private?

Yes. ueue stores everything locally on your device. There’s no account and nothing is uploaded to a server, so your saved links stay yours.

Why doesn’t a thumbnail show for some links?

Some sites (like Instagram) don’t allow their previews to be fetched, so ueue shows a clean type icon instead of scraping them — by design, to respect your privacy and their terms.
