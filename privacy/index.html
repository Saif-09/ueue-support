Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

The short version: ueue is a fully on-device app. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. Everything you save — links, categories, sessions, reminders — stays on your device. There are no accounts, no servers we operate, no analytics, no advertising trackers, and no third-party SDKs that phone home with your data.

ueue ("the app") is built and operated by Saif Siddiqui ("we", "us"). This policy describes how ueue handles your information on both iOS and Android.

What the app stores

The app stores the following locally on your device, in a private database accessible only to the app:

  • Links you save through the system share sheet or in the app
  • Categories you create
  • Scheduled sessions and reminder times
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device. We have no way to access it.

Permissions the app uses

Capability Why
Notifications Show the local reminders you scheduled inside the app.
Exact / time-sensitive alarms Fire one-off reminders at the exact time you chose. Falls back to a standard notification if unavailable.
Run after reboot (Android) Re-register your scheduled reminders after a restart so they still fire.
Internet Fetch link previews for URLs you save. Used only for previews — no analytics or tracking calls.

None of these are used to collect or transmit your personal data.

Sharing with third parties

We do not share any data with third parties, because we do not collect any data to share.

When you open a saved link, your device hands it to the destination app (YouTube, your browser, etc.). That app's own privacy policy governs what happens after the hand-off — ueue is not involved.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from anyone. As stated above, the app does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to this policy

If material changes are made, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new version will be published at this same URL.
